Day 1

Burj khalifa

829.8 meters is the tallest building in the world and the most famous of the city's points of interest. For most visitors, a trip to the observation deck on the 124th floor here is a must-do while in the city. The views across the city skyline from this bird's-eye perspective are simply staggering.

Day2

Desert Safari

In the afternoon, you will be taken to the desert with photo-stops in an exciting dune drive. Proceed to the camel farm and explore great pleasure. Continue your journey towards the desert and watch the beautiful sunrise in the spot. Reach our campside and enjoy the camel ride, sand boarding and henna design on your hands or feet. After a great day, enjoy a delicious barbecue dinner and a shisha (the famous Arabic water pipe). Later, watch out our belly dancer performing her show around the campfire by starlight.

Day3

Burj Al-Arab

The Burj Al-Arab is the world's tallest hotel, standing 321 meters high on its own artificial island on the Dubai coastline. the way to experience the over-the-top opulence is to go for dinner at the underwater Al-Mahara restaurant, where floor-to-ceiling glass panels in the dining room walls allow you to view sea life while you eat, or you can enjoy lunch at California-style fusion restaurant Scape.

Day4

wild wadi water park

Dubai's famous man-made island development - is a great place to cool off after a few days of hectic shopping and sightseeing. The waterslide action here is excellent and world-class, with the Aquaconda, the world's longest water slide; a nine-story-tall slide, aptly named the Leap of Faith; and water coaster rides.
